## Full Description (max 4000 characters)

CelebConnect makes sure the people who matter to you always feel remembered. Add birthdays, anniversaries, and any special occasions once — and let CelebConnect automatically send warm WhatsApp messages on the day, every year.

**What CelebConnect does:**
• Add birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and custom events to your personal calendar
• Write personalised message templates with {name} placeholders for each person
• Auto-send WhatsApp greetings at your chosen time each morning
• Set reminders 1–7 days before events so you never get caught off-guard
• Open Instagram profiles instantly to post a story or send a DM
• Manage recurring events — yearly, monthly, weekly, or one-time
• Works offline — notifications and event management work without the internet

**Why CelebConnect:**
People don't forget birthdays because they don't care — they forget because life is busy. CelebConnect runs quietly in the background, then handles the celebration so you show up like the thoughtful friend you are.

**Privacy first:**
Your data stays yours. We never share your contacts or messages with advertisers. See our full Privacy Policy at [link].
